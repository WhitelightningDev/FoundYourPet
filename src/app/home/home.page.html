How It Works

Step 1: Register Your Pet

Sign up and create a profile for your pet, including all necessary contact information.

Step 2: Attach QR Code

Receive a personalized QR code that you can attach to your pet's collar.

Step 3: Get Notified

If your pet is found, the finder scans the QR code and contacts you directly through our platform.
